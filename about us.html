HISTORY


We are a friendly running group based in Hammersmith Road, London and are part of an affiliated athletics club - New Lane City Athletics Club.Our club was started in 20th October, 1990 and the founders of this club are, Mr. Ankur Lamichhane and Mr. Alex JB Rana."Live for health, Run for health" is the primary moto of this club. This club not only provides trianings but also provides runners with various oppurtunities. Our club has been organising racing competitions and motivating runners to take part in the program. The secondary moto of our club is to maintain healthiness among the people and to prepare them mentally for taking part in the social activities without hesitation.

About Us


The club is open two eveneings a week i.e. Tuesdays and Thursdays.Runners here are divided into three(3) different groups-Beginners, Intermediates and Advanced. Training courses vary between groups. Training routes are also different in different groups. Runners are allowed to choose their routes and also are allowed to choose the race events they are interested in.
