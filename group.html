Groups of our club

Our club has three different groups. Runners are allowed to choose their own groups. One runner can be attached with only one group. The groups available are Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced. Register here to join our group.

Beginner Group:

Our club provides training as per the groups. More effort is needed in beginner group so trainings are provided respectively. Beginner group has different running routes and different training palce. The entry fee for the beginner group is $2000( for yearly participation ) and $200 (for monthly paticipation).

Intermediate:

The entry fee for the Intermediate Group in our club is $150(for monthly participation) and $1500 (for yearly participation). Runners from this group are provided with 3-4 miles of training routes. We provide you the best facility and make your career better.

Advanced:

Advanced group posse our faith and promises. All the people joining this group tend to be the best of the bests. The fee for this group is :
$100-for monthly participation.
$1000 for yearly participation.
We provide this group with the trainings routes upto 10-miles. Runners here will learn to apply more effort for. near future
